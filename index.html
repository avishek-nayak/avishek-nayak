Hi, I am Avishek Nayak, with 4 years of experience that began in a

— Design studio where most discussions revolved around design guidelines, learning design systems, variables, auto-layouts, advanced prototyping, and more.

— Then moved to a service company working directly with clients, understanding their business, improving user retention rates, building monetary features, disagreeing with developers and project managers on design decisions, and handing off design files.

In these 4 years, I learnt a lot about what actually goes into design, why a non-linear design process rarely works in the real world, and why some designs aren't feasible for certain tech stacks. Evolving through this, I'm now on a journey to join as a Product Designer.
